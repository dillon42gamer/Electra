Tips

  1. At the Bug Hunt level, your missiles can hit a large area. To destroy several targets, open fire, even without waiting until you accurately put the missile on the target.
  2. It is much easier to destroy the enemy with missiles, rather than shoot them with guns. Aim at the right and then launch the rocket.
  3. At the Maze mission level, try to keep below the canyons so that enemies can not find you. 4. At the Zero Gravity level, do not go too far to the ground, otherwise you will not be able to complete the task.
  4. Choose a suitable aircraft for the mission. For some battles you need to choose a large aircraft, and for missions at low altitude, small, smart cars are better suited.
  5. In conventional air battles adhere to the recommended altitude, but do not crash into protruding forms of relief, otherwise death is inevitable.
  6. Do not save, release a couple of extra missiles better to destroy the enemy with a guarantee. You, as a rule, have enough, and misses are always possible.
  7. During an air duel, use air brakes reasonably. You can fly to the enemy in the rear and shoot him from the cannon.
  8. You can easily dodge the enemy rocket, turn off the "afterburness", look at the radar and quickly turn aside.
  9. Aim at the air target, determine it, and when you hear the target-lock tone, press "afterburness" and fire.
  10. High-altitude Blackbird does not work very well in the lower atmosphere. Keep high in the blue skies and avoid clouds.

General tips for passing

  • The plot branches out into several options, the choice of which depends on the "Decision Points" section that appears after every few stages. Depending on the option you choose, you will be in favor of one or another corporation. By default, you start the game for UPEO.
  • There is a difference between "Mission Accomplished" and "Mission Over" - very important. If at the end of the mission you see the message "Mission Over", it means that you could not cope with all the tasks in this mission, get the rank of "D", or lower. Messages received in the course of the mission will be activated in memory and later will help to use various tricks.
