Descent (DOS)

Genre: Action
Perspective: 1st-person
Gameplay: Arcade, Shooter
Interface: Direct control
Vehicular: Space flight
Setting: Sci-fi / futuristic
Published by: Interplay Productions
Developed by: Parallax Software Corp.
Released: 1995
Platforms: DOS, PlayStation (PSX, PS1)
Station «Shiva».
Earth's orbit.
The Company's management extraterrestrial minerals.

With the principal's office and start a new game company «Interplay». First-savers, we learn that it it will be about space exploration and combat with extraterrestrial civilizations, cunning and evil aliens. For those who love space strategy, waiting for a big disappointment. However, those who are at the heart of space shooters, - too. The authors have created a very unusual game, a sort of synthesis of the labyrinth adventure in the style of «Doom» and space simulator battleship. Unconditional resemblance to «Doom» is that the action of the game takes place in mines and tunnels scared Rudnikova labyrinth, and problem plays is that, having shot all in its path (except the captured enemies hostages), do some basic mission. Image synthesized in such a way that we look at what is happening with their own eyes. With space simulator «Descent» kinship that move in the maze is not walking (old-fashioned), and in the cockpit of a fighter ship ultramodern PYRO-GX Israeli production, equipped with the very latest military hardware. But the main thing - is not it. Basic similarity with simulators is that we are completely free in the movement, because we fly. Limit our ability just comes down mines. But they, bizarre twists, leaving it down sharply, then - steeply, to diversify (and complicate) the slip in space. Our ship can move freely, not only forward, backward and sideways. He can go down and up, obeying only the will of the pilot, without paying any attention to the laws of gravity, or the steepness of the floor of the mine.
In the end, that had become a largely traditional and popular genre among the people, stood before us in a different light. In this case, complicated, by making full use of the space, «Descent» will be difficult to pass even tried «DOOM» fighters.
On the advantages and disadvantages of graphics will be discussed later, but for now - back to the story, to the office of Director of the Company extraterrestrial minerals, and continue the conversation earlier. By the way, you - fighter pilot, and set up a very, very pessimistic. But what to do? You are in the service. I have to listen and obey.
The problem faced by your company, is this - mines on Pluto have been someone else's attack. Defensive robots do not work. The most vulnerable spot mines - reactors that are at great depths, from orbit to destroy not possible, as all the attacks were repulsed from space. You are proud of, believe the only one in this situation will not only help the Company and all earthlings. Yes, the issue has been that way. By itself, the loss of mines unpleasant, of course, but the main point is that the ultimate goal of invading - Earth. And we assume, of course, we can not.
Your main goal - to blow shaft reactors, and with them, and all the infected mine. Along the way you'll need to destroy enemy robots in an amount that is within your reach and sufficient to perform basic tasks. In addition, you will need to release captured by the aliens of our employees, who worked in the mines. Henceforth lives of the hostages will be in your hands. Then - as the data about the invasion scrappy and not systematic, you will carry out video of enemy combat vehicles for transmission of films to the appropriate services. Finally, the last, the most important - you will still survive. But not because they love you so much (after all, you're working for money). Just missions similar to the first, a lot, and you - one. And save the humans and the Company appears to be no one else. Therefore, the reactor blew up, you should always have time to sneak out through the emergency exit.

Consolation can serve only three things.

First. Your PYRO-GX is not just made with the latest technology. He is also equipped with weapons and equipment that is no longer used anywhere, but, and this is absolutely true - a good and powerful.
Second. Our last remaining intact robots had secretly smuggle in weapons to mine new PYRO-GX and necessary equipment. So in the mines you will not feel completely alone. The center will periodically remind yourself various upgrades of your ship.
And finally, the third - the enemy is still no one disturbed and sudden attack can succeed.
What you will find in the depths of the captured enemy mines?
Primary information is small and sketchy. In infected mines - robots. Most of them are made on the basis of standard mining machinery. There is, however, a completely new model. All that has penetrated into the mine, has adapted very quickly. Internal camera not working, but our companions found several modified robots closer to the surface.

Practical tips

  • To fight the enemy robots using the entire width of the mines. Blows your ship on the wall does not bring PYRO-GX is no harm;
  • As soon as possible include cruising mode. This allows you to not be distracted by the extra buttons, fully concentrating on the study of mazes and fight with the enemy;
  • As often as possible include the view from the cab back. The ego will allow you to notice the approach of a particularly insidious enemy robots;
  • Use in combat all the advantages of three-dimensional solution of the game. Dodge the rockets and missiles not only in the side, but also up and down;
  • Receiving a new weapon, expend it sparingly. Remember that you are on the threshold of the unknown. Who and what is waiting for you at the back - no one knows, and the presence in the arsenal of the most powerful weapons never hurt. Protect the missile - in times of crisis, they will be your only salvation;
  • For all types of shooting missiles beware. Unlike guns, missile warheads, torn on the wall near your vehicle, you will be systematically applied considerable damage;
  • Collect all in the maze, you see. Shells, energy charges, protective screens - it's all there in the mines and will greatly help you to survive;
  • Pick up your liberated hostages;
  • Do not miss out on any door. Many of these "open" a good rocket salvo. Usually for these doors are very useful things;
  • After the loss of one life, you continue to play with the full protection of the maximum possible at the moment with all the weapons and found the keys. So, dying once - take heart. Still to come;
  • Carefully study the map of the dungeon. Despite its seeming uncomfortable, it is indispensable for the successful solution the problem;
  • Remember your first goal - the keys, then - the emergency exit, and then - the reactor. Moreover, in order to get to it, do not necessarily have all the keys. The path to victory is much shorter than it seems;
  • After the explosion of the reactor (the best - missiles) you will have a few seconds, then, to be saved. Proceed quietly, ignoring the roaring sirens and rushing around the enemy robots. Do not be distracted by them - after the explosion, and they will die without your help.
