Road Rash (3DO)

Genre: Action, Racing / Driving
Perspective: Behind view

Released: 1994
Platforms: 3DO Interactive Multiplayer, PlayStation (PSX, PS1)

It all began on Sega, when in 1991, from the restless depths of the game, the first game of the series, still gaining momentum, was released. And, as they say, she shot. Control keys

Before the start of the competition, use the arrow buttons to view the motorcycles, use A, B or C to select. Press L and R to set volume.

On the road:

  • ⇦, ⇨ - sharp slopes;
  • A - to brake;
  • Ð’ - accelerate;
  • B, B (fast) nitro boost;
  • C - attack (blow by hand);
  • ⇧ + C - kick from behind;
  • ⇓ + C - kick;
  • L / R - smooth slopes.

In the Der Punzer Klub:

  • Schmooze - another biker;
  • Bulletin Board - selection of the next race.

Rest room - setting options:

  • Game mode - Trash (arcade training) or Big (big race).
  • Player mode - competition with the clock or with rivals.
  • Player level - the choice of one of five levels of complexity in the Trash-mode,
  • Engine sounds Race Music - on/off technical noise and music.
  • Jukebox - music player;
  • The Street - walk along the street;
  • Olley's Skoot-A-Rama - the choice of a motorcycle (rat, sport, super).

Tips

  • The easier the motorcycle you choose, the easier it will be for them to drive on the street.
  • Try not to travel to unpaved roads.
  • On prize money, buy your motorcycles and weapons more powerful.
  • In fights with brothers, rockers, use both hands and feet, and chains with a club ... Try to drive your opponent to the lane that the truck is rushing towards: you will like it.
  • Try not to run into puddles of oil, as well as water.
  • To get to the next level, you need to come in the top three.
  • Businessmen. On the third or higher level, on the track "The Peninsula", race to the mark of 6.6 on the speedometer - and you will see two businessmen with faces of clowns crossing the road. Try to hit or at low speed to move one of them.
  • Off-road. On the track "Napa Valley" at the first fork (at the mark of 1.9 miles) turn right - and you will roll down the country road.
  • Walking without motorcycle.Reset the speed-to zero. Press L + R + ⇧ and do not let your rider jump off the motorcycle and will wander around the neighborhood on foot.
