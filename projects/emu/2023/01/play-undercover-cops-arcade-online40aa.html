Undercover Cops (Arcade)

Genre: Action
Perspective: Side view
Visual: 2D scrolling
Gameplay: Beat 'em up / brawler
Narrative: Crime
Published by: Irem Corp.
Developed by: Irem Corp.
Released: 1992
Platforms: Arcade Machine, Super Nintendo (SNES)

An unusual arcade beat 'em up, which takes place in a dystopian future, the surroundings are more reminiscent of the Middle Ages, if you do not pay attention to the occasional advanced military equipment. You play as a police group of three powerful fighters, clearing the streets of New York from criminal punks and cyberbosses behind them with the most brutal methods.
